Usage Guidelines

✓ DO

  • Use approved color combinations only
  • Maintain proper clearspace and margins
  • Preserve aspect ratios when scaling
  • Use high-resolution files for print
  • Ensure adequate contrast on backgrounds

✗ DON'T

  • Alter or modify brand colors
  • Stretch, skew, or distort logos
  • Add unauthorized effects or shadows
  • Place on busy backgrounds without proper contrast
  • Use low-resolution files for professional applications
